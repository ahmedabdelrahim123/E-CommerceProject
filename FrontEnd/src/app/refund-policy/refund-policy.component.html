Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from our website. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we will be happy to provide a refund or exchange for the item(s) you purchased.

Eligibility for Refunds and Exchanges:

  • To be eligible for a refund or exchange, you must contact us within 14 days of receiving your order.
  • The item(s) must be in their original condition and packaging.
  • You must provide proof of purchase, such as your order number or receipt.
  • We reserve the right to refuse a refund or exchange if the item(s) are not returned in their original condition.

How to Request a Refund or Exchange:

To request a refund or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. We will provide you with instructions on how to return the item(s) to us.
  2. Once we receive the item(s), we will inspect them to ensure they are in their original condition.
  3. If the item(s) are in their original condition, we will process your refund or exchange within 14 days.
  4. Please note that we do not refund shipping costs.

Exceptions:

There are some items that are not eligible for refunds or exchanges. These include:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items that have been used or damaged
